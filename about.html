About InduSustain Systems

We exist to help industrial organisations move from reactive maintenance and siloed systems to connected, intelligent plants powered by digital twins.

InduSustain Systems brings together expertise in embedded systems, industrial IoT and cloud platforms to create virtual replicas of your physical assets. These digital twins allow you to see how machines are performing now, how they are likely to behave in future and what interventions will deliver the best outcome.

We work across the full stack – from PCB and controller selection, firmware design and industrial networking, through to data pipelines, cloud architectures and visual dashboards. The aim is simple: give your teams trustworthy, real-time insight so that downtime and waste become the exception, not the rule.

Our delivery model is collaborative and practical. We start with one or two critical assets or lines, prove value quickly, then scale to fleets, plants and networks with reusable design patterns.
